About

Hello! My name is Corey and I enjoy bringing ideas to life through creating code. My interest in Web Development started back in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. What started off as playing around with ideas on WordPress turned into a passion for writing clean code.

Fast-forward to 2022; with the help of great mentors and completion of entry tests, I was accepted into Hack Upstate's Third Cohort for Careers in Code. Third Cohort was a rigorous 24-Week program where we learned the basic fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript and applied what we learned to a series of projects and a capstone, which was presented at graduation.

Upon completing the program in September of 2022, I am now preparing to start my journey into the Tech Industry as a Front-End Developer.
